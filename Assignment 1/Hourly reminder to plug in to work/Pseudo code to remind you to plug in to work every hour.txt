Pseudo code that reminds you to plug in to work every hour.
STEP 1: Start
STEP 2: Initialize hour, minute and seconds.
STEP 3: Sync digital reminder or alarm clock with processor speed
STEP 4: Set hourly interval 
STEP 5: Set count timer to begin at zero minute
STEP 6: If timer count = 60 minutes
STEP 7: Display reminder with alarm, then restart timer count at zero minute.
STEP 8: Else, keep timer counting and reconnect to STEP 6.
STEP 9: Stop.
NB: Reminder words = Plug in to work.
